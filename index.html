This site previously hosted a scam stealing cryptocurrency wallets. However, it has been reported as a phishing site to the runcitadel team. If you have been redirected to this site by @AaronDewes, it was very likely a fake account and not the real person. Please let us know in that case so we can try to prevent further scams.
